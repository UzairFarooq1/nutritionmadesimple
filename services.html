Services

What We Do

Weight Management Programs

Designed weight loss or weight gain programs tailored to your needs, incorporating both diet and lifestyle recommendations.

Nutritional Counseling

We Offer one-on-one consultations to discuss and address clients' unique health concerns and challenges and Provide guidance on making healthier food choices and developing sustainable eating habits.

Nutrition Workshops & Seminars

I Conduct educational sessions for groups, schools, or community organizations to raise awareness about the importance of good nutrition

Follow-Up and Support

I Offer ongoing support and follow-up appointments to track progress, make adjustments to nutrition plans, and address any challenges clients may encounter.

Specialized Diets

Provide guidance on specific dietary patterns based on individual preferences and requirements.

Nutrition Education

Conduct workshops or seminars on various nutrition topics, helping clients understand the fundamentals of good nutrition and its impact on health.

How it works

Find below a breakdown of our services and associated pricing:

Initial Discovery Call

FREE This is a 15 to 20-minute call that will be via telephone.

Initial Consultation

This consultation lasts for 45-60 minutes and can be conducted virtually via telephone or video call for £75, or face-to-face at our private clinic in Leicester for £95.

This package includes three 30-minute consultations. The virtual package costs £150, while the face-to-face package at our private clinic in Leicester costs £200.

Private Clinic is based in Leicester close to A47, approximately a 20-minute drive from Leicester city center.
